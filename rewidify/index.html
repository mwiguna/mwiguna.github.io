    Getting Started
    Use Meta Tag Viewport in the Tag Head of your HTML files : 
    <meta name="viewport" content="width=device-width, initial-scale=1.0">
    The meta tag viewport going to make your browser can detect the window size.
    Then Download and include rewidify.css/rewidify.min.css into your files : 
    <link rel="stylesheet" type="text/css" href="rewidify.css">
    Customize
    Rewidify has 12 columns. You must include class="col-*" into class="row". see the code below : 
    
<div class="row">
    <div class="col-12">Column 12</div> 
    <div class="col-11">Column 11</div>
    <div class="col-10">Column 10</div>
    <div class="col-9">Column 9</div>
    <div class="col-8">Column 8</div>
    <div class="col-7">Column 7</div>
    <div class="col-6">Column 6</div>
    <div class="col-5">Column 5</div>
    <div class="col-4">Column 4</div>
    <div class="col-3">Column 3</div>
    <div class="col-2">Column 2</div>
    <div class="col-1">Column 1</div>
</div>

    Grid Options

    You may use more options columns for different window sizes :
    >= 610px
    Small Devices
    Smartphones col-xs-*
    >= 740px
    Small Devices
    Tablets col-sm-*
    >= 840px
    Medium Devices
    Tablets col-md-*
    >= 990px
    Medium Devices
    Desktops col-lg-*
    >= 1180px
    Large Devices Desktops col-*

    Each grid options has 12 columns.

    Container

    You may use class="container" : 
    
<div class="container">
    <div class="row">
        <div class="col-8">Column 8</div>
        <div class="col-7">Column 7</div>
        <div class="col-6">Column 6</div>   
    </div>
</div>

    Push

    You may use class="push-*" to push your column (make empty space on the right side) : 
    
<div class="row">
    <div class="col-8 push-2">Column 8</div>
    <div class="col-7 push-1">Column 7</div>
    <div class="col-6">Column 6</div>   
</div>
    There are 12 push classes to make empty space.

    Offset

    Offset is the opposite of the Push. Offset useful for push your column from the left side (make empty space on the left side) : 
    
<div class="row">
    <div class="col-8 offset-2">Column 8</div>
    <div class="col-7 offset-1">Column 7</div>
    <div class="col-6">Column 6</div>   
</div>
    There are 12 offset classes to make empty space as well.
